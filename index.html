Hey, I'm Nik, an Occupational Therapist based in Belmont, Perth WA. I work at the intersection of soft tissue rehabilitation and neurodiversity-affirming practice, with a genuine passion for gaming-informed approaches that meet clients in the spaces they already feel comfortable.

My approach is simple: trust comes first, therapy second. Whether we're working through pain and movement limitations or building independence and daily function, I take the time to understand your world before we start working on it together.

I trained and completed my degree in Occupational Therapy and have been in clinical practice for just over a year, bringing fresh, evidence-based thinking and a whole lot of enthusiasm to every session. Alongside my independent practice, I work at Estus Health as part of a team dedicated to neuroaffirming care across Perth and telehealth Australia-wide.

Outside the clinic, I host OT & Yap, a podcast connecting OTs and allied health professionals across Australia. Always happy to connect.

Soft Tissue Therapy

Evidence-based manual therapy designed to address the physical barriers keeping you from daily life, conducted from my dedicated treatment space in Perth.

  • Myofascial release
  • Range of motion support
  • Pain & tension management
  • Functional recovery focus
🌿

Occupational Rehabilitation

The OT difference, treatment isn't just about how you feel in session, it's about returning to your routines, hobbies, work, and self-care with real independence.

  • Functional goal-setting
  • Activity & occupation analysis
  • Return to daily routines
  • Self-care & independence
  • NDIS & private bookings
🧠

Neurodiversity Support

Strengths-based, affirming therapy for neurodivergent individuals, using gaming-informed approaches and evidence-based strategies to build regulation and independence.

  • Sensory regulation strategies
  • Executive function support
  • Gaming-informed approaches
  • Adolescents & young adults
📍 Location note: Soft tissue sessions are conducted from my private residence in Belmont, Perth. Community and home visits are available for OT and neurodiversity support only (NDIS clients), subject to location. Reach out and we can work out what suits you best.
